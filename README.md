# Marcos — Interactive Bilingual Bible Study Tool

An interactive single-page tool for studying the Gospel of Mark in Spanish (NTV) alongside the Greek.

- Click any word for grammar analysis and conjugation tables
- Audio pronunciation (Latin American Spanish)
- Tutor chat panel for follow-up questions
- Color-coded verb tenses

Currently covers Marcos 1:1–3. Goal: extend to all of Mark.

## Sources

- **Greek:** Aland, K., Black, M., Martini, C. M., Metzger, B. M., Robinson, M. A., & Wikgren, A. (1993; 2006). *The Greek New Testament, Fourth Revised Edition (with Morphology).* Deutsche Bibelgesellschaft.
- **Spanish:** *Nueva Traducción Viviente* (2009). Tyndale House Publishers, Inc.
